About MathChampion

Empowering the next generation of mathematical minds through competitive excellence

Our Mission

To inspire and challenge students worldwide through innovative mathematical competitions, fostering critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a lifelong appreciation for the beauty and power of mathematics.

Our Vision

To be the world's premier platform for mathematical excellence, where every student can discover their potential, push their boundaries, and contribute to the global community of mathematical thinkers and innovators.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to global recognition

2020

The Beginning

MathChampion was founded by a group of mathematics educators and enthusiasts who believed that competition could inspire excellence in mathematical thinking.

2021

First Contest

Our inaugural contest attracted 150 participants from 25 schools, establishing the foundation for what would become a global phenomenon.

2022

International Expansion

We welcomed our first international participants, with students from 12 countries joining the competition, truly making it a global event.

2023

Digital Innovation

Launched our online platform, making the contest more accessible and introducing real-time scoring and interactive problem-solving features.

2024

Record Participation

This year, we're proud to host over 500 participants from 30+ countries, with our largest prize pool yet and enhanced learning resources.
